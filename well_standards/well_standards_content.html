California Well Standards, Combined
This online publication is an informal compilation of Bulletin 74-81 and the draft supplemental, Bulletin 74-90. Although Bulletin 74-90 was not finalized by DWR, nor officially adopted into the Model Well Ordinance by the SWRCB, it has been adopted into local ordinances around the state. To facilitate its use, DWR combined the contents according to pages 9 - 10 and Table 1 of Bulletin 74-90.
