Appendix B

REFERENCES

Since Bulletin 74-81 was published in mid-1981 several new or revised publications have been issued that address groundwater or well construction. This appendix lists publications issued or revised since 1981 and selected other publications that were reviewed during the preparation of this supplement. Publications that were used for Bulletin 74-81 that have since been revised are identified by a number in parentheses. These numbers refer to the publication’s original position in the biography of Bulletin 74-81 (Appendix E).
